Who we are?

A modern professional services firm committed to excellence

We are a modern professional services firm committed to delivering high-quality, timely, and pragmatic financial solutions to individuals, startups, SMEs, and large corporates. Anchored by deep domain expertise in accounting, auditing, taxation, and financial advisory, we enable our clients to effectively navigate complex financial, regulatory, and compliance landscapes with confidence and clarity.

Backed by a cumulative professional experience of over 25 years, the Partners operate through two distinct yet synergistic entities: Patil Wafekar & Co. LLP, Chartered Accountants, and Patil Wafekar Consultancy LLP.

Our core values are founded on Professional Excellence, Integrity, and a Client-First Philosophy. We adopt a solution-driven and risk-aligned approach, leveraging technology-enabled workflows, robust governance frameworks, and data-driven insights to deliver value-centric outcomes, support informed decision-making, and drive long-term sustainable growth for our clients.
